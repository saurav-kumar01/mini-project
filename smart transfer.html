Only the receiver can see your files

Only you and the receiver can access your files in Smart Transfer. Your data is encrypted end to end, and can only be read by your receiver (and you of course)

No file size limit

There's no limitation on the size of the files you can send, nor the amount. You are only limited to what your device (or browser) can handle. To ensure maximum performance make sure to keep your browser up-to-date.

Better for the environment

Because we don't store your data we use almost no power!. By using Smart Transfer you'll have a much smaller carbon footprint than when using a cloud storage provider

Nothing stored online

When the transfer is complete your files are no longer on the internet, so there's no risk of anyone hacking a server to get to your files: it's simply not there. We always find the shortest path from sender to receiver meaning sometimes your data doesn't even have to leave your network!
